Q3 2012 Growth Goals Dashboard

Goal Definitions

Focus Goal
Awareness 6M unique visitors in September. (Progress on this goal must be monitored through Google Analytics-- it is not covered by this page.)
Registrations 400,000 new user registrations in September.
Long-term users. 300,000 users who performed an activity in September that was at least 30 days after their registration date.
Engagement 150,000 users who performed an activity on four or more days in September.
Note: For the 3rd and 4th goals, an "activity" is currently defined as any of the following: starting a video, attempting a problem, or posting a comment, question or answer. This definition will change over time to include more diverse activities, but will not change for the calculation of Q3 goal performance.
